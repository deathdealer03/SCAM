Project Overview

Protecting India's Job Seekers with AI

ScamShield is an AI-powered fake job and internship detection system built by Team-J at Graphura India Private Limited. With millions of young Indians actively searching for employment, fraudulent job postings have become a critical threat — stealing money, personal data, and time from vulnerable job seekers.

Our system combines Natural Language Processing (NLP) with structured Machine Learning to analyze job postings across 4 dimensions simultaneously: keyword patterns, domain trust, salary anomalies, and language model scoring. The result is a real-time risk score with explainable indicators.

Natural Language Processing

NLP Pipeline

How raw job text is transformed into fraud probability

🧹
01

Text Preprocessing

Lowercasing, stop-word removal, punctuation stripping, and Unicode normalization on the raw job description text

📐
02

TF-IDF Vectorization

Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency converts text to a numerical feature matrix. Captures word importance relative to the training corpus

🤖
03

Logistic Regression

Trained on 5,500+ labeled job postings (scam / genuine). Outputs a fraud probability score (0–1) for any new posting

🔀
04

Weighted Fusion

NLP score is combined with keyword, domain, and salary scores using calibrated weights to produce the final risk score
