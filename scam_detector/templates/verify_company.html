👤 Recruiter Verification Guide

Trust Indicators
✅ Verified Recruiter Signs
  • • Official company email domain
  • • LinkedIn profile with history
  • • Verified company on Glassdoor
  • • Internshala / Naukri listing
  • • Clear interview process
⚠️ Suspicious Signs
  • • WhatsApp-only communication
  • • Gmail / Yahoo email for HR
  • • No verifiable company address
  • • Vague job description
  • • Pressure to join instantly
🚨 Blacklisted Signs
  • • Registration / training fee demand
  • • Security deposit required
  • • Asks for Aadhaar/PAN upfront
  • • No physical address or website
  • • Unrealistic salary promised
